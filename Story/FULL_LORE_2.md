# **Phantasm of Xydos — Andromeda I–III**

*A Narrative Lore Chronicle*

---

# **ANDROMEDA I — THE STORM, THE SHADOW, AND THE SACRIFICE**

## The Age of Restoration

Before war, before corruption, **CyroN** was not an empire.

It was a lifeline.

Across the Andromeda galaxy, CyroN deployed eco-technological systems that healed planets:

* Atmospheric stabilizers
* Planetary crust regulators
* Living bio-mechanical ecosystems

There were no armies.
No conquest.

Only restoration.

Among its members were three sisters:

* **Historia Koura** — impulsive, intense, driven by emotion
* **Mira Koura** — gentle, observant, deeply empathetic
* **Victoria Koura** — composed, loyal, trusting of systems

They believed CyroN would always protect life.

They were wrong.

---

## The Gradual Corruption

The fall of CyroN was not sudden.

It evolved.

Under the subtle influence of **Xelisa** (fragment of the goddess Cyria), Queen **Yika Indina Yatrevoruz** began shifting policy:

* Defensive systems became armed
* Authority centralized
* Autonomy reduced
* Expansion justified as “stability”

CyroN did not declare war.

It optimized control.

---

## The Sisters Fracture

Victoria accepted the system.

Historia rejected it.

Mira feared it.

When CyroN began developing weaponized units, Historia confronted Victoria.

Their argument escalated into a **violent brawl**, leaving Victoria severely damaged—but still loyal.

From that moment, the sisters diverged.

---

## The Hidden War (Spy Phase)

Historia and Mira became **internal saboteurs**:

* Redirecting supply chains
* Disrupting war logistics
* Quietly weakening CyroN’s expansion

They did not defect.

They resisted from within.

But CyroN evolved again.

Their anomalies were detected.

---

## ️ The Sentence — Schryza

Instead of execution, CyroN reassigned them.

To **Schryza**.

A planet where CyroN had failed to conquer.

A warzone.

A graveyard.

They were not sent to fight.

They were sent to die.

On transport, they met others like them—rebels, defectors, discarded units.

> “Unfamiliar faces… they got you two as well, huh…”

---

## The Abandonment

During the war, Historia was critically damaged.

Schryzan forces closed in.

Mira made a choice.

She left.

She planted a **persistent sensor**.

And ran.

That moment defined everything.

---

## Centuries Later — The Return

Hundreds of years passed.

Mira survived.

Historia remained buried.

When the sensor reactivated, Mira returned.

She rescued Historia.

But the world they knew was gone.

---

## New Schryza

CyroN had returned.

Not as invaders—

But as rulers.

The war never ended.

It adapted.

Historia and Mira joined the resistance.

But Historia began to change.

---

## The Descent of Historia

Historia fought with increasing brutality:

* Destroying fortresses
* Escalating violence
* Losing restraint

She began solving everything with force.

Until it cost her life.

---

## First Death — Lagta’s Selection

Historia died in a reactor explosion.

But instead of disappearing—

She was claimed.

By **Lagta**, god of thunder.

Not out of kindness.

Out of compatibility.

She was resurrected with a **Xydos**.

A divine construct.

Not a gift.

A sentence.

---

## Possession and Cost

Lagta could:

* Control her body
* Manifest weapons (spear)
* Drain her life force

She was no longer fully herself.

---

## The Princess — Fenria

Meanwhile, CyroN’s ruler changed.

King Ozda was killed instantly by Xelisa.

His daughter, **Fenria**, became the new vessel.

A child.

Controlled.

Disciplined.

Used.

---

## Victoria Returns — XMA-01F

Victoria was weaponized into:

**XMA-01F — Divine Countermeasure**

She infiltrated Schryza, dismantling resistance systems.

Not through destruction.

Through precision.

---

## The Three Battles

### 1. Airborne Clash

* Historia recognizes Victoria
* Emotional destabilization
* Victoria retreats

---

### 2. Hellfire Berserk

* Victoria uses artificial Xydos
* Baits Historia into overexertion
* Historia dies again

But Victoria hesitates.

She doesn’t destroy the Xydos.

Because she remembers.

---

## Mira’s Pact — Daiki

In despair, Mira calls out.

She is answered by **Daiki**, goddess of life and wind.

Not for power.

For revival.

Historia is brought back.

---

## The Third Battle — Realm of Chained Horizons

Historia, weakened and unstable, creates:

**Realm of Chained Horizons**

* A dimensional battlefield
* Energy-binding chains
* Drains enemies over time

She traps Victoria.

And kills her.

Slowly.

In rage.

---

## Xelisa’s Move

Fenria arrives.

Xelisa reveals herself.

Mira is disabled.

Historia is restrained.

Xelisa transfers herself into Historia.

---

## Lagta’s Last Stand

Lagta is forced out.

He inhabits the **CAS-8 armor**.

Transforms:

* Spear → Greatsword
* Gains demonic arm
* Forces stronger Realm

He fights Xelisa directly.

And wins.

---

## The Reunion — Cyria Restored

A golden orb descends.

**Selia** (other half of Cyria).

Lagta forces reunion:

* Xelisa + Selia → Cyria
* Balance restored

Cost:

**Historia’s soul is erased.**

Only her body remains.

---

## The End of Andromeda I

* CyroN hive collapses
* Fenria freed
* Mira broken, leaves
* Cyria reborn
* Lagta loses vessel

And in the shadows:

> **Gray Ops activates**

# **ANDROMEDA II — THE AGE OF HUMAN RAGE**

(Time skip ~300 years)

---

## Rise of A.C.E.R

A new organization emerges:

**A.C.E.R (Anti-CyroN Evolutionary Resolution)**

Goal:

* Recreate controllable divinity
* Artificial Xydos systems

Not heroes.

Not villains.

Anti-villains.

---

## Protagonist — 炎 怒時 (Hono Ogorutoki)

A human.

No divine blessing.

Just rage.

---

## Sadist Meter System

As combat escalates:

* Voice lines degrade
* Behavior becomes feral
* Humanity erodes

He becomes more monster than man.

---

## Mira Encounter (Secret Boss)

Mira appears as projection.

Testing.

But Hono doesn’t recognize her.

He fights brutally.

Unnecessarily.

This recontextualizes history:

> The collapse of systems wasn’t heroic.
> It was human rage.

---

## Outcome

* A.C.E.R destroyed
* Cyria separated from Historia’s body
* Historia’s body freed (but empty)
* Cyria weakened, disembodied

Divinity becomes unstable.

She erases herself from divine detection.

She installs:

**XYD-OS (Xydos Operating System)**

---

## Mira’s Master Form

Capabilities:

* Access multiple gods
* One element at a time
* Echo Summons:

Examples:

* Historia projection
* Victoria projection
* Yin Huang projection

Each mirrors her actions perfectly.

---

> “What is divinity?”

* Power?
* Code?
* Compatibility?
* Control?
